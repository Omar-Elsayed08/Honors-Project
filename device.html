UP-811 First Medical Device

40 years after founding, in 1986, Sony develops its first medical device, an A6 ultrasonic diagnostic printer with medical capabilities. Sony did not start producing medical technologies right away as they were founded. They had developed other types of technology for 40 years before they entered the medical field. The UP-811 was created for doctors to view inside their patient's bodies and make an accurate diagnosis. It works similar to an X-ray device. This device could view the organs and bones of a patient from the outside without hurting the patient. The UP-811 was designed to be easy to use and incredibly helpful. It also came with network capabilities to transmit the medical data securely and safely. Collaboration between medical professionals became easier and more common. It was imperative for the UP-811 to send a message that Sony is committed to developing helpful technologies for the betterment of everyone's health. The success of the UP-811 revealed the future potential success of Sony.
