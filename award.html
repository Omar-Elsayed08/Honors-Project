2020-Sony Receivs an Award for it's Medical Technology

Sony's mSafety won the "Best Biometric Sensor Solution" in 2020. The award was received at the MedTech Breakthrough Awards. They topped 80 different companies and claimed this prestegious award. This honor solidified Sony as a true giant in the medical technology field. Sony's mSafety was a revolutionary biometric sensor and was a huge breakthrough for the capabilities of biometric sensors in the future. Biometric sensors identify physiological characteristic of individuals for identification or authentication. Coupled with its function in emergency response situations, mSafety claimed this award at the MedTech Breakthrough Awards for it's innovative functionality and use. Sony's mSafety solution can serve as a foundation for further development in other medical devices ad fields. The mSafety device can also be useful in other industries and can be used to create more functional devices in those respective fields.
